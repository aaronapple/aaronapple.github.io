Hello

Trained as a cognitive scientist, with expertise in language learning, I'm looking to transition into the applied learing sciences. My goal is to improve learning outcomes by disseminating and applying findings from the developmental sciences to influence education policy.

I grew up outside Chicago, my mom teaches art history and my dad is a programmer for an information storage company. I moved to the east coast for college, and as much as I love it out here, I miss the windy city. I'm a recent graduate of Johns Hopkins University's Department of Cognitive Science.

Education

Johns Hopkins University

M.A. in Cognitive Science

Graduated: July 2016

My graduate research focused on how language processing behaviors such as reading, understanding, and producing language are influenced by linguistic experience, over the course of a few milliseconds or over many years.

New York University

B.A. in Language and Mind

Graduated: December 2012

I started out expecting to study comparative literature and explore my interest in archetype realization across the folklores of different cultures, but I ended up with a bachelor's degree in Language and Mind, a joint major in the departments of Psychology and Linguistics.
